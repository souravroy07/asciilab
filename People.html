Principal Investigator

Dr. Avishek Adhikary

Associate Professor, Department of Electrical Engineering

Dr. Adhikary directs the ASCII Lab, focusing on applied sensing, intelligent control architectures, and the deployment of autonomous systems in complex environments. His research bridges theoretical mechatronics with scalable industrial and defense applications.

Notable Student Projects

PhD, MTech, and BTech Theses

Consensus Framework and Flocking Theory for Multi-agent Systems

Developed distributed control algorithms enabling coordinated formation and flocking behavior across multiple autonomous agents, addressing communication delays.

Swaninda Ghosh, Rohit Bose BTech Thesis

Fractional Order Colpitts Oscillators for Bio/Chemical Sensors

Designed and implemented a modified fractional-order oscillator specifically tuned for highly sensitive impedimetric analysis of biological excretions.

Agniv Tapadar PhD Thesis

Fungal Disease Detection via Plant Pathological Scale

Utilized advanced image classification techniques and CNN architectures to automate the severity assessment of early blight in agricultural crops.

Niladri Biswas MTech Thesis

ExoSkeleton Design and Control

Ongoing research focused on the electromechanical design and real-time kinematic control systems for assistive human-robot interaction devices.

Yogendra Kumar PhD Research

Biomedical Feature Extraction: ECG and Cell Malignancy

Designed data-analysis-based fractional order notch filters to isolate highly accurate diagnostic features from noisy biomedical signals.

Aashuli Sahare MTech Thesis

Nondestructive Ripeness Detection System using Computer Vision

Developed a machine-learning-aided optical system for the real-time classification and ripeness grading of bananas in supply chain environments.

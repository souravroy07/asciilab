Advancing Autonomous Frontiers

ASCII Lab conducts interdisciplinary research bridging intelligent sensing, robotics, computer vision, and cyber-physical technologies. We are dedicated to formulating robust solutions for real-world engineering challenges.

Our core focus encompasses the development of intelligent robotic systems specifically engineered for underwater inspection, structural health monitoring, and advanced autonomous navigation using state-of-the-art machine learning frameworks.

Mission

To engineer intelligent, reliable, and scalable sensing and control architectures for autonomous systems operating in complex environments.

Vision

To establish a world-class center of excellence driving indigenous innovation in intelligent mechatronics and AI for India.

Research Domains

Pioneering advancements across interconnected disciplines to develop resilient cyber-physical systems.

Underwater Robotics

Development of Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROV) and AUVs for inspection, featuring subsea localization and structural health monitoring frameworks.

Computer Vision

Leveraging deep learning architectures (like YOLOv8 and DenseNet) for robust digital image processing, crack enumeration, and object detection in highly degraded visual environments.

Unmanned Aircraft Systems Initiative

Development and integration of advanced payload sensing and autonomous flight control algorithms for Unmanned Aircraft Systems. This principal project involves designing robust embedded frameworks for real-time data acquisition and tactical path planning in varying environmental conditions.

View Details
Institute Funding Ongoing

Underwater Metal Corrosion Detection using FIFISH V6 Expert

Establishing an experimental framework utilizing the FIFISH V6 Expert underwater drone to construct high-fidelity datasets within controlled environments. The project focuses on deploying machine learning pipelines to detect and quantify metal corrosion and structural defects autonomously.

Lab Timeline

The latest achievements, milestones, and updates from ASCII Lab.

June 2026

Experimental Dataset Construction Completed

ASCII Lab has successfully concluded Phase 1 of our underwater experimental data collection. Utilizing a custom-fabricated volumetric glass tank and the FIFISH V6 Expert drone, the team has curated a high-fidelity dataset for training robust structural inspection and metal corrosion detection models.

Research Update
May 2026

New Manuscript Submitted to IEEE Sensor Letters

Our latest research titled "Real-Time Water and Surface Classification and Crack Enumeration on Underwater Metallic Structures," spearheaded by PhD Researcher Sourav Roy and Dr. Avishek Adhikary, has been submitted for peer review. The paper details a novel methodology utilizing YOLOv8 and DenseNet-201 architectures for degraded aquatic environments.

Publication
March 2026

MeitY Funds Unmanned Aircraft Systems Project

ASCII Lab is proud to announce a new research grant funded by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY). The project focuses on "Capacity Building for Human Resource Development for Unmanned Aerial Systems." A recruitment drive for a Principal Associate position was successfully conducted.

Funding & Grants
February 2026

Strong Presence at IEEE APSCON 2026

Multiple researchers from ASCII Lab presented their findings at the IEEE Applied Sensing Conference (APSCON). Presentations included innovative work on disposable sensors for rapid impedimetry and the integration of LDA-SVM for fluid adulteration detection.

Conference
September 2025

ASCII Lab Officially Inaugurated at IIT Bhilai

The Applied Sensing and Control for Intelligent India (ASCII) Lab was formally established in the Department of Electrical Engineering at IIT Bhilai under the direction of Dr. Avishek Adhikary, paving the way for advanced mechatronics and AI research.

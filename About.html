Laboratory History & Philosophy

Located within the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bhilai, the Applied Sensing and Control for Intelligent India (ASCII) Lab was established to pioneer indigenous advancements in cyber-physical systems, applied mechatronics, and artificial intelligence.

Under the leadership of our Principal Investigator, Dr. Avishek Adhikary, ASCII Lab has evolved into a dynamic hub for interdisciplinary research. We are driven by the ambition to bridge the gap between theoretical sensing paradigms and deployable, real-world autonomous systems.

Research Philosophy

At ASCII Lab, we view complex engineering environments—such as highly degraded underwater conditions or dynamic aerial airspaces—not as limitations, but as essential parameters for robust system design. Our methodology embraces iterative empirical testing and data-driven learning. We firmly believe that overcoming experimental anomalies leads to the most resilient technological solutions.

"Education is a progressive discovery of our own ignorance. "

Mission

To engineer intelligent, reliable, and scalable sensing and control architectures for autonomous systems (AUVs, ROVs, UAVs) operating in complex environments, integrating advanced machine learning pipelines directly into embedded edge devices.

Vision

To establish a world-class, globally recognized center of excellence that drives indigenous innovation in intelligent mechatronics, robotics, and cyber-physical technologies for a self-reliant India.

Infrastructure & Facilities

ASCII Lab is equipped with cutting-edge hardware, custom experimental rigs, and high-performance computational resources to accelerate complex mechatronic and AI-driven experiments.

Underwater Experimental Setup

Dedicated bespoke facilities engineered for submerged structural health monitoring, fluid-dynamics observation, and underwater computer vision dataset generation.

  • FIFISH V6 Expert Underwater Drone (ROV)
  • Custom-fabricated volumetric glass observation tanks
  • Subsea illumination arrays and specialized optic sensors
  • Real-time surface classification testbeds

UAV & Autonomous Systems

Comprehensive hardware suite for algorithmic testing of path-planning, payload management, and autonomous flight dynamics.

  • Multi-rotor Unmanned Aircraft Systems (UAS)
  • Advanced FPGA & Embedded Controller Development Boards
  • LIDAR mapping and Depth-Sensing Modules
  • IoT interface gateways for swarm communication

Computational Intelligence Hub

High-throughput computing infrastructure dedicated to training deep learning models and executing complex simulations.

  • High-end GPU workstations (optimized for YOLOv8 & DenseNet)
  • Digital Image Processing and annotation stations
  • Hardware-in-the-Loop (HIL) simulation environments
  • Software suites: MATLAB, Gazebo, ROS2 integration
